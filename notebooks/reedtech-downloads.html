Patent Grant Single Page Images - Yellow Book


The Yellow Book 2 archives are six types of files:

  • grant_yb2_yyyymmdd.tar:  This is the main weekly grant file. Contains the images of each patent grant issued weekly (Tuesdays) from July 31, 1790 to present in Tagged Image File Format (TIFF) Revision 6.0 with CCITT Group 4 Compression (single-page TIFFs) from the USPTO Patent Grant Image/TIFF (a.k.a., Grant Yellow Book) product. Approximately 4,000 patent grants per week.
    Documentation:  https://www.uspto.gov/products/xml-resources.jsp
  • grant_yb2_yyyymmdd_rx.tar:  A "new" separate file for Reexaminations.
  • cofc_yb2_yyyymmdd.tar:  Certificates-of-Correction.
  • cofc_yb2_yyyymmdd_pta.tar:  Patent Term Adjustment (PTA) Certificates-of-Correction.
  • grant_yb2_yyyymmdd_r1.tar:  Replacement documents from a given Tuesday issue.
  • grant_yb2_yyyymmdd_cert.tar:  Patent Grant Certificates (daily).
  • Bnnnnn.tar:  Rescans of miscellaneous documents from miscellaneous years (1790 – present). The filename relates to the Digital Linear Tape (DLT) cartridge or optical disc.
